About Landscape

We're building the platform that ARGUS should have built 10 years ago.

The Problem

Commercial real estate runs on spreadsheets duct-taped to institutional software that hasn't meaningfully evolved in two decades. ARGUS serves the top of the market at institutional prices. Everyone else exports to Excel and prays the formulas don't break. The industry deserves better tools — tools that actually understand how deals work across property types, that can read your documents, and that give you real answers when you ask questions about your model.

Built by a Practitioner

Built by a CRE practitioner with 35 years of experience — 8 years as a commercial appraiser, then co-founding two active real estate investment companies and serving as principal in 60+ residential subdivisions and master-planned communities totaling 20,000+ lots.

Landscape isn't an academic exercise or a tech company's idea of what real estate needs. It's built on the frustrations, workflows, and hard-won instincts of someone who has actually underwritten, developed, and managed deals across every property type the platform supports.

Alpha Program

Landscape is currently in private alpha with a select group of investors, developers, lenders, and appraisers. We're looking for practitioners who want to shape the platform — people who are tired of the status quo and willing to test, push back, and help us build something that actually works for how CRE professionals think.
